# Research Questions

## Objective

The purpose of this project is to evaluate the quality of biomedical responses generated by large language models using a structured scoring framework.

---

## Primary Research Questions

1. Which biomedical domains achieve the highest evaluation scores?

2. Which evaluation dimension contributes most to lower overall performance?

3. Are there recurring weaknesses across different biomedical prompts?

4. Which response characteristics are associated with higher risk levels?

5. Does factual accuracy correlate with the overall evaluation score?

---

## Secondary Research Questions

- Are terminology errors associated with lower clarity?

- Do certain biomedical domains consistently produce stronger responses?

- Which evaluation dimension shows the greatest variability?

---

## Expected Outcome

The analysis will identify strengths, weaknesses, and recurring error patterns in biomedical LLM responses, providing a reproducible framework for quality assessment.
